About Us

A brief History

Funding Hub was founded on the belief that everyone deserves a fair shot at getting a fair loan. We pride ourselves on shopping a huge network of 200+ lenders to help you get a loan that works best for your situation.

We want to put the power back into the hands of the consumers by giving them a centralized location to receive multiple loan offers by filling out one simple form. Our goal is to make it as easy as possible to get a great loan at a great price and get on with your life.

We also look to help you understand and unlock your credit potential and lower the cost of your credit.We hold ourselves to the highest standard: If it isn't a loan we would do ourselves or recommend to our family and friends, we don’t recommend it to you.

Funding Hub is a leading online loan marketplace with one of the largest networks of lenders in the nation. Our service provides consumers a way to connect with multiple lenders for a number of financial borrowing needs. From the comfort of their homes, consumers can fill out one simple form and are able to shop, compare, and save on the loans they need.



Who are we

We're founded by a team of financial service professionals that pride themselves on helping consumers reach better outcomes. We're math majors, finance majors, software majors and have optimized wealth management experiences, lending experiences and even apparel ones!

The advice we give you is based on many hours of data crunching and research and validated by $100's of millions in loans for people just like you. We want you to find a loan, get approved and have the experience be so easy that you want to tell your friends about it.
